Biography

Charlie Lehman is a Machine Learning Ph.D. student at the Georgia Tech Omni Lab fro Visual Engineering and Science (OLIVES). His research interests include robustness and explainability of deep vision models. He is also an Engineering Duty Officer with the U.S. Navy Reserves, where he specializes in the maintenance and repair of surface vessels.
